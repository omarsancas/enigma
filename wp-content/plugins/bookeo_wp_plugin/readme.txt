Bookeo lets your customers book appointments, classes, tours, events directly in any page of your web site.

== Description ==

Bookeo lets your customers schedule appointments with you online, 24x7. It offers advanced features such as online payments and deposits, gift vouchers, promotions, memberships, and much more.
Bookeo offers three different products, sharing the same goal: simplify online bookings and payments for businesses, and help them grow their revenue by effectively using social and viral marketing.

* Bookeo Appointments, designed for consultants and therapists that take individual appointments.
* Bookeo Classes and Courses, geared towards teachers and schools that want to manage their class scheduling and enrollments.
* Bookeo Tours and Activities, designed for guided tour companies and providers of outdoor activities.

**Main features**

* full integration in your web site and facebook page
* accept online payments and deposits
* offer extras and options
* offer gift vouchers, promotions, prepaid packages
* manage memberships and recurring payments
* GroupOn/LivingSocial integration with automatic voucher validation
* 2-way, hands-free sync with your Google Calendar
* iPhone, Android, Outlook calendar sync
* staff and vehicle rostering
* send email and sms reminders
* promote viral marketing by your customers
* track customer history, preferences
* multilanguage
* integration with Google Analytics and custom conversion tracking tools

Bookeo can be embedded directly into your web site, so your customers never leave your site.
Bookeo is simply unmatched in flexibility: you can add custom options to the booking form, define different prices for different times of the day, weekends, high season, group size, and so on. You can easily add pictures and videos to build a media gallery.

Finally, Bookeo lets you analyze your business with reports, trend analysis and charts. Add a 'book now' to your web site today, and start accepting online bookings!

== Installation ==
